How this works:

  • This is a site for buying and selling anonymously and without censorship.
  • All transactions are stored on the Ethereum blockchain. Less crucial data (images) are stored on IPFS (torrents).
  • The site does not generate any profit or revenue. Any transaction fees go to the Ethereum network to secure transactions.

    • To buy or sell on this site you need:
    1. Secure email address.
      • Go to protonmail.com to set up a secure, anonymous, end-to-end encrypted email address.
    2. Ethereum account with ether which is not associaed with your identity.
      • Go to www.myetherwallet.com to create an ethereum wallet.
      • Download the metamask extension for your browser. Link it to your wallet.
      • Anonymously move some ether to the wallet.
        • a. Use a mixing service, or
        • b. Buy peer-to-peer with a site like localcryptos or kijij.
    3. Metamask: extension can be downloaded for most major browsers.
    4. VPN recommended

    Why use this site?
  • Code is on the blockchain and cannot be shut down
  • Transactions are anonymous if proper care is taken

    • Seller:
  • Register as seller and provide a valid email
  • Post item for sale. Price to include shipping. Images to be .jpg format.
  • Posting typically costs around 0.01 eth ($1.20 as of March 2020)
    • When a seller purchases your product, the transaction will show up under LIST MY ITEMS as requiring action.
    • Also, when a seller purchases, he may email you, referencing the transaction number from his registered email
    • At this point, the seller and buyer must arrange for shipping and delivery

    Buyer:
  • Click on item to view.
  • Click to buy (will verify via Metamask). This requires entering either mailing address or email.
  • Buying fee is approx 0.01 eth ($1.20 as of March 2020). This goes to the ethereum network, not the site.
  • Contact seller at email provided to arrange for shipment / delivery.
  • Please confirm receipt and rate seller after receiving product. This helps the site function.

    • Warning:
  • The site is trust-based. If a seller does not deliver, the site cannot be held responsible.
  • Please take care to buy from sellers with a high rating and a large number of sales.
